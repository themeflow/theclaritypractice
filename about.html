The Belief Behind the Practice

We believe the most significant challenge for any leader is not a lack of information, but a lack of clarity. In a world of constant noise, the ability to see clearly, think strategically, and act decisively is the ultimate competitive advantage.

About Gary McRae

The Clarity Practice was founded by Gary McRae, an executive coach and consultant with a singular focus: helping senior leaders find the clarity they need to solve complex challenges, inspire their teams, and lead with quiet confidence.

After years in high-stakes corporate environments, Gary recognized that the most successful leaders were not those with the most answers, but those who could ask the most powerful questions. They possessed an ability to cut through complexity and focus on what truly mattered.

This insight led to the creation of The Clarity Practice—a firm dedicated not to providing solutions, but to building the capacity for clear thinking within leaders themselves.

Gary is a certified Practising Management Consultant (PMC) with the Singapore Business Advisors and Consultants Council (SBACC), a credential recognized by Enterprise Singapore. This qualification underscores his expertise in business strategy and his ability to guide SMEs in leveraging government grants for capability development.

His approach is built on the Three-Pillar Clarity Method™, which integrates the grounded presence of Mindfulness, the rigorous logic of Strategic Thinking, and the insightful synthesis of Visual Thinking. This unique blend moves leaders from a state of mental clutter to one of focused, sustainable clarity.
